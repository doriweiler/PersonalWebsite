Hi, I'm Dori Weiler!

From iteratively improving educational programs to exploring AI’s implications for learning, I’m an assessment specialist and learning science researcher who loves asking tough questions, digging into messy data, and distilling complexity into meaningful stories that drive real change.

  • My Story

The WHY Behind My Work

As AI rapidly transforms education, I’m passionate about helping educators and institutions use evidence as a compass— translating research into practice while keeping student learning outcomes at the center.

Learning Science

Applying cognitive science and evidence-based pedagogy to strengthen learning, feedback, and course design.

Assessment

Designing outcomes-aligned assessments and rubrics that make student learning visible, measurable, and actionable.

AI in Education

Evaluating AI tools and developing learning-centered guidance that prioritizes educational quality and integrity of evidence.

Bringing a Unique Perspective

With a PhD in animal behavior and ecology, classroom teaching experience, and a background in learning assessment, I bring a unique perspective that combines scientific rigor with a commitment to improving learning through evidence-based design.

  • Learn More

AI For Learning

I’m passionate about AI’s potential to expand access, personalize learning, and support instructors– while ensuring implementation stays grounded in evidence, equity, and real learning outcomes.

  • Learn More

Research Foundations in Animal Behavior

My Doctoral training is in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology, where I trained as a scientist studying complex systems, behavior, and causal relationships in messy real-world environments. That research training now shapes how I evaluate learning: rigorously, skeptically, and with deep respect for evidence.
